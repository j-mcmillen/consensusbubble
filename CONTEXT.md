# Consensus Bubble

A community organiser where every change — settings, details, content, membership, moderation — happens only through consensus. Communities are Bubbles that nest fractally into larger Bubbles; consensus forms bottom-up.

## Language

**Bubble**:
A community of ~10 Members (or, at higher levels, ~10 member Bubbles) with its own settings, details, and Wall, governed entirely by consensus.

**Member**:
A person belonging to a Bubble. At higher levels of the fractal, a Bubble is itself the member of its Superbubble.

**Proposal**:
The only instrument of change. A suggested change to a Bubble's settings, details, Wall content, membership, or anything else, judged by one Voting Window. It is Open from submission until it becomes Passed, Failed, or Withdrawn.
_Avoid_: motion, request, submission

**Proposal Policy**:
The parameters governing Proposals against a given target (today: Voting Window length), resolved most-specific-first — per-subject, then per-kind, then the Bubble default. Policies are themselves settings, changed by consensus.

**Voting Window**:
The fixed period a Proposal is open for Vetoes, its length taken from the target's Proposal Policy (Bubble default 48 hours). The window always runs to expiry; the outcome is judged only at expiry.
_Avoid_: poll, ballot, deadline

**Veto**:
The only kind of vote. A Member's objection to a Proposal, always carrying a comment explaining it. A Veto stands until its caster Retracts it; it survives Adjustments.
_Avoid_: no-vote, downvote, objection

**Self-Veto**:
A proposer vetoing their own Proposal — holding it open for discussion and Adjustment with no risk of it passing. The system's de-facto draft state.
_Avoid_: draft

**Retraction**:
A vetoer withdrawing their own standing Veto while the Voting Window is open.

**Adjustment**:
The proposer revising a live Proposal in response to Veto comments. An Adjustment restarts the full Voting Window but leaves standing Vetoes in place.
_Avoid_: amendment, edit

**Withdrawal**:
The proposer cancelling their own live Proposal before expiry. No consensus is recorded.

**Consensus**:
A Voting Window expiring with zero standing Vetoes. Silence is unconditionally consent: Members who do not act are consenting, with no quorum or participation requirement.
_Avoid_: approval, majority, passing vote

**Resubmission**:
A fresh Proposal (with a fresh Voting Window and no carried-over Vetoes) made after a previous Proposal failed at expiry.

**Discussion Thread**:
The conversation attached to every Proposal. Comments there never factor into the consensus outcome — only Vetoes do.
